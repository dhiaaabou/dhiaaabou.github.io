PORTRAY is a mobile Art app specific for photographers and painters, where you can share, find an art course, and follow the art events. A great way to stand out on PORTRAY is to share the artist's experience by creating his gallery or organising new events or following it. That is, the application will help in more than one field, it will help promote the artist's works, increase his followers, reach his works to a large segment of people, and organize special exhibitions for his works to allow the opportunity to sell them, especially since the application can be developed through updating it and adding online marketing. On the other hand, the application is very important to provide the opportunity for talented or people who are looking for courses in the field of photography and drawing to find suitable courses for them, just the user needs to click on the option of the course, choose the course and apply. The platform is built around content and community – making it a great idea for artists to engage with fans. People love to see the artist behind the work, and relate with the artists on a more personal and casual level by sending a direct message or chatting with them if the artist is online.
